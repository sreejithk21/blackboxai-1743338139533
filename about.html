Our Story

Founded in 2015, ScrapeHero began as a small team of data enthusiasts who recognized the growing need for reliable web data extraction services. What started as a side project quickly grew into a full-fledged business as more companies discovered the value of web data.

Over the years, we've refined our techniques to handle increasingly complex scraping challenges while maintaining high ethical standards. We're proud to serve businesses ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies across various industries.

Our team combines expertise in web technologies, data engineering, and machine learning to deliver solutions that go beyond simple scraping. We're constantly innovating to stay ahead of anti-scraping measures and provide our clients with the clean, structured data they need.
