Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my page limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. Additional pages are charged at $0.01 per page for Basic and $0.005 per page for Pro plans. Enterprise plans have no overage charges.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 7-day free trial for our Basic and Pro plans. No credit card required to start the trial.

How is billing handled?

We bill monthly via credit card. Annual billing options are available with a 10% discount.

What if I need custom features?

Our Enterprise plan includes custom development. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
